Welcome to the NMSCD

No Man's Sky Community Developers & Designers

Why does this exist?

A lot of the tools that exist for No Man's Sky are isolated. The creators have done amazing jobs working on their projects. But we could do so much more together! We are a collection of Developers, Designers, etc... all focused on making NMS more enjoyable for its players.

Join us!

What are we working on?

One of the first projects started in the NMSCD is the No Man's Sky - Universal Font started by YakuzaSuske and then extended by Blend3rman. This project aims to give Developers and Designers a beautiful NMS themed font, fixing many of the imperfections of the nomanssky.com version.
This site uses the Universal NMS Fonts!
